Welcome to Vetplayas Veterinary Clinic

Advanced yet affordable pet care! At Vet Playas Animal Hospital and Pet Spa, we know how important your pet is to you. That's why we're committed to providing the best veterinary care for your pet.

Vet Playas Animal Hospital offers a full range of veterinary services designed to provide the highest level of care for your special pet. Our facility is complete with surgery, radiology, dentistry and an in-house laboratory. In addition to general medicine.

Our staff is dedicated to providing the highest quality veterinary medical care for your pet. This Web site is another way for us to provide the information you need to make informed decisions about the health and well being of your pet.

As a full service companion animal pet hospital we offer a full veterinary laboratory, pet surgery, a pet pharmacy, heartworm, and tick - flea control. We understand the special role your pet plays in your family and are dedicated to becoming your partner in your pet's health care. Our goal is to practice the highest quality veterinary medicine and pet surgery with compassion and an emphasis on client education.

Our pet healthcare team is committed to providing personal attention to the unique concerns of each individual pet owner and to the needs of each pet. At this site, you will find information about our practice philosophy, our services, helpful forms to assist you and an extensive Pet Medical Library for you to search for additional pet health care information.
